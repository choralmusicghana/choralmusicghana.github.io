Ghana's Favourite Love Song Competition

As part of Choral Music Ghana's drive to inspire more diverse expression in Ghanaian art music, we are happy to invite all Ghanaian composers to our first nation-wide competition for Ghanaian composers: the Ghana's Favourite Love Song Competition!

The focus of this initiative is to broaden the scope of musical expression available to the listening public for their appreciaiton. By being a patron of the ground-breaking, Choral Music Ghana hopes to provide more opportunities for composers and musicians to explore the possibilities of their art in an encouraging space.

Competition Rules & Guidelines

You must submit an original composition for two voices and piano. The words of your composition must be yours, and the arrangement must be for male and female duet, accompanied by a piano. Stylistic choices are at your discretion, although preference will be given to submissions that take advantage of the idioms unique to Ghanaian art music.

To participate, follow these simple instructions:

  1. Submit the original score and lyrics in an email as two separate PDF documents, and an audio recording of the work for evaluation. The recording must be of two singers being accompanied by a keyboard. You may submit an amateur recording.
  2. The quality of your recording will not influence the decision of the judges.
  3. Your submission can be in any language.
  4. Your submission must include your full name, address and preferred contact number.
  5. Email your submission to submissions@choralmusicghana.com with the subject “Ghana’s Favorite Love Song Competition.
  6. Resubmissions are permitted. However, only the last submission will be valid.
  7. Follow Choral Music Ghana's social media channels for updates on the competition.
  8. All submissions ​must be made by the 14th of January, 2018.

Evaluation

A panel of critics will evaluate each work anonymously and present a shortlist of finalists. The finalists will be presented to the public for voting. The winner of the poll will be presented with a cash prize and other recognition paraphernalia.
