The Best of Ghanaian Art Music

Choral Music Ghana is the most respected promoter of Ghanaian art music on the Internet.

We're a registered musical arts and culture non-profit dedicated to Ghanaian musicians and their work. Choral Music Ghana devotes itself to the appreciation of Western classical music expressed by Ghanaians in keeping with the European tradition while maintaining its essential, authentic african point of view.

We aim to be the world's authority on Ghanaian art music by providing a critical perspective of the flowering music scene in the country, while encouraging more people to participate in this unique cultural expression.

We are a group of enthusiastic musicians, writers and critics who volunteer our time and talents to bring the best of Ghanaian art music to the world.

Inspiring Initiatives

Concert & Album Reviews

Since its inception, Choral Music Ghana has published some of the best and most widely read concert reviews. These reviews highlight significant events in Ghana's music space, as well as provide insight into the work of some of Ghana's most innovative talents.

The Ghana Music Directory

Our growing directory of musicians past, and practicing, is meant to draw attention to the highest profile individuals who are creating the sounds we love to hear. The directory also focuses on ensembles, from choirs to symphony orchestras, to raise more awareness of their activities inside and outside the country.

Work with Us!

Choral Music Ghana is run by a body of volunteers who reach out to the world to make Ghanaian choral and classical music known and appreciated. We put our talents to use by initiating projects that bring value to the lives of musicians and enthusiasts like us.

We're looking for passionate individuals to help promote Ghanaian choral and classical music. If you want to join our body of volunteers, send us an email at choralmusicghana@gmail.com and introduce yourself. We'll love to work with you!
