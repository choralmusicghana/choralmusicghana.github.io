The Ghanaian Music Database

As part of our drive to make Ghanaian art music more accessible, we have opened to the public our searchable archive of original compositions by Ghanaian musicians past and present. This archive represents music that is currently in the public domain.
Among the works availabe here are those by illustrious composers and rising stars within the country. To make use of this database, enter the name of any popular Ghanaian composer, or song/piece title. All available scores are in PDF.
